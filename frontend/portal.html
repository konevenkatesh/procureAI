Draft a new tender

3-gate workflow: brief → fact verification → clause review → final approval. Powered by the procureAI Drafter LangGraph (M3) at .

Step 1 — Project brief

Paste a free-text description OR upload a DPR (PDF / DOCX / Markdown). The LLM will extract project facts you'll review at the next step.

Supported: PDF (Docling-converted to MD before extraction), DOCX, Markdown, plain text. File is stored in /tmp on the server during the session.

Step 2 — Confirm Project Facts

Review the facts the system extracted from your brief. Fields with a red border are REQUIRED and must be filled. You may override any extracted value the system got wrong.

Step 3 — Review Selected Clauses

The system has selected clauses based on your project facts. Most officers will click Accept All here. To drop a specific clause, expand the section below and toggle it off.

Step 4 — Final Approval

The draft has been assembled and validated. Review the validation report below and the document preview. Approve to finalise; revise to send back for changes.
